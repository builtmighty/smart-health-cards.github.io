What is a SMART Health Card?

SMART Health Cards are trustworthy, verified versions of your clinical information, such as vaccination history or test resultsm, that contain a secure QR code. They may be saved digitally or printed on paper and shared with others if you choose.

This website will help you get a SMART Health Card, update an existing SMART Health Card, or learn more about the benefits of SMART Health Cards and how they work.
